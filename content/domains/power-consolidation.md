Power consolidation tracks how influence and control concentrate within technology markets and the institutions that govern them. Sieve scores articles on this domain when they involve:

- **Monopoly dynamics** — Market dominance, anticompetitive practices, and winner-take-all effects
- **Regulatory capture** — When the entities being regulated influence or control their regulators
- **Vertical integration** — Companies expanding to control entire supply chains or ecosystems
- **Gatekeeping** — Platform control over who can access markets, distribute content, or reach audiences
- **Institutional capture** — Tech industry influence over government, academia, and media

Articles scoring high on Power Consolidation often cover Big Tech antitrust actions, platform policy changes that affect smaller competitors, acquisitions that reduce market diversity, and the revolving door between Silicon Valley and government.
