The attention economy describes the competition for human attention through technological systems designed to capture, hold, and monetize it. Sieve scores articles on this domain when they involve:

- **Behavioral advertising** — Targeting systems that profile users to serve personalized ads
- **Algorithmic curation** — Feed algorithms that optimize for engagement over information quality
- **Engagement optimization** — Design patterns (infinite scroll, autoplay, notifications) engineered to maximize time-on-platform
- **Attention extraction** — Business models where user attention is the primary product being sold

Articles scoring high on the Attention Economy domain often cover social media platform changes, ad-tech developments, content recommendation systems, and the growing tension between user wellbeing and engagement metrics.

Every article Sieve processes receives a score of 0–3 on this domain. A score of 3 means the article is deeply relevant to attention economy dynamics. Browse articles scored on this domain in the [Reader](/reader/?axioms=attention_economy).
