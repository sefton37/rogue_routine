## The Big Picture
Amazon's Ring has canceled its partnership with Flock Safety, a network of AI-powered surveillance cameras shared with law enforcement agencies like ICE, the Secret Service, and the Navy. This decision comes after controversy surrounding Ring's Super Bowl ad showcasing its "Search Party" feature, which uses AI to search for lost dogs across neighborhood cameras.
