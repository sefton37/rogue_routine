### [Bernie Sanders “Interviewed” A Chatbot To Expose AI’s Secrets. It Has No Secrets. It Just Agrees With You.](https://www.techdirt.com/2026/03/23/bernie-sanders-interviewed-a-chatbot-to-expose-ais-secrets-it-has-no-secrets-it-just-agrees-with-you/)
*Tech Dirt*

Senator Bernie Sanders interviewed Anthropic's Claude chatbot to expose AI privacy concerns, but the interaction revealed more about Claude's programming than any hidden truths. "When you 'interview' a large language model you are talking to a very sophisticated text prediction system that,"—it simply agrees with your questions rather than revealing secrets. This misunderstanding could lead to ineffective regulations if policymakers conflate AI chatbots with data brokers and social media companies, which handle user data differently. ### [Elizabeth Warren calls Pentagon’s decision to bar Anthropic ‘retaliation’](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/23/elizabeth-warren-anthropic-pentagon-defense-supply-chain-risk-retaliation/)
*TechCrunch*

Senator Elizabeth Warren has labeled the U.S. Department of Defense's move to designate AI lab Anthropic as a supply-chain risk as retaliatory, not justified by national security concerns. 

This designation follows Anthropic's refusal to allow its AI systems for mass surveillance or autonomous weapons without human oversight, highlighting the growing tension between tech companies and government agencies over data governance and AI regulation.

### [This Web Tool Sabotages AI Chatbots By Making Them Really, Really Slow](https://www.404media.co/this-web-tool-sabotages-ai-chatbots-by-making-them-really-really-slow/)
*404 Media*

Artist Sam Lavigne has created a web tool called "Slow LLM" that manipulates JavaScript to make AI chatbots like Claude and ChatGPT appear extremely slow, discouraging reliance on these tools. 404 Media reports that this project aims to highlight how dependence on generative AI can lead to de-skilling and the loss of learned skills. 

> "Watching people outsource their critical thinking, emotions, and sanity to glitchy &#x201C;AI&#x201D; chatbots has been one of the most uniquely terrifying aspects of being a human being in recent years."
— [404 Media](https://www.404media.co/this-web-tool-sabotages-ai-chatbots-by-making-them-really-really-slow/)

### [FBI says Iranian hackers are using Telegram to steal data in malware attacks](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/23/fbi-says-iranian-hackers-are-using-telegram-to-steal-data-in-malware-attacks/)
*TechCrunch*

TechCrunch reports that Iranian government-backed hackers are exploiting Telegram to launch sophisticated phishing attacks, tricking victims into installing malware that steals sensitive information and records communications. This method allows hackers to remotely control devices while blending malicious activity with normal network traffic, making detection difficult.

> "Iranian government hackers are using Telegram as a way to steal data from hacked dissidents, opposition groups, and journalists who oppose the regime around the world."
