*Tech Dirt*

Why is Congress considering an extension of surveillance powers without necessary reforms? Tech Dirt reports that despite previous commitments to address civil liberties concerns, current House Speaker Mike Johnson plans to move a clean extension of Section 702 of the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act (FISA) for at least 18 months without additional safeguards or oversight. This decision disregards earlier efforts to negotiate warrant requirements and other critical changes aimed at protecting Americans' privacy.
